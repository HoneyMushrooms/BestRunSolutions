Client Information

Overview

SAP will end mainstream support for ECC ERP by 2027, prompting many companies to migrate to SAP S/4HANA. This transition offers significant modernization opportunities, as S/4HANA is built on the in-memory HANA database, supporting advanced technologies like AI. The shift reflects the need for more agile, scalable systems to meet modern business demands, since ECCâ€™s aging framework limits its ability to adapt to current and future needs.

Tcell successfully migrated its ERP system from SAP ECC to SAP S/4HANA in partnership with Best-Run Solutions. The project lasted 6 months and included modernization key business processes, implementation of the newest SAP solution and corporate data migration.

4

5

6

Training the end users on the new version

7

Support during the testing process

Project results

Thanks to automation and AI, SAP S/4HANA streamlines many business processes. This improves efficiency, reduces redundancy, and provides a better customer experience. All of this can reduce business costs and boost revenue.

Main benefits of moving to SAP S/4HANA for Tcell include:

  • Better integration with other SAP products;
  • Lower costs, such as lower operational and ownership costs;
  • A smaller database footprint due to simplified data models;
  • Automated processes improving productivity and performance;
  • Faster, more intelligent decision-making;
  • Greater user and business satisfaction.
